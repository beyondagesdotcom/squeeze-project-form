Our Charity Partner

Meet SCOPE

The national nonprofit behind The Squeeze Project's mission — sending kids to summer camp, and walking with them long after the summer ends.

What SCOPE Does

A pathway to potential — through camp and beyond

SCOPE is a national nonprofit funder of overnight summer camp and college scholarships, creating pathways to potential through Camp, Classroom, College, and Career for children and teens from under-resourced communities who commit to staying in school.

SCOPE stands for Summer Camp Opportunities Promote Education.
The Path

Camp · Classroom · College · Career

Camp is just the beginning. Through SCOPE, kids don't just experience one summer of opportunity — they embark on a journey that keeps them on track for success in school, college, and beyond.

01 · Camp

Camp

Immersive summer experiences where children develop social, emotional, and leadership skills.

02 · Classroom

Classroom

Every SCOPE camper commits to staying in school, applying the resilience and confidence gained at camp to their academic pursuits.

03 · College

College

Scholarships and support services open doors to higher education.

04 · Career

Career

Mentorship and networking prepare young adults for successful professional lives.

What SCOPE Believes

Every kid deserves the chance

The convictions that drive SCOPE — and the reason The Squeeze Project chose them as our charity partner.

That all children deserve the chance to experience summer camp to explore, learn, and laugh with friends from sunup to lights out.
That summer camp is where kids discover that they can.
That meeting new people at camp helps children feel like they can belong just about anywhere.
That conquering challenges — on a ropes course or in life — teaches kids to aim higher.
That making a splash in life can start with doing cannonballs off a dock.
That the great outdoors is the greatest classroom.
That every child should know there's s'more to life out there, and everything they try, discover, and conquer at camp creates a path to even greater possibilities in school, college, their careers, and beyond.
The Squeeze Project × SCOPE

Why we partnered with SCOPE

×

The Squeeze Project exists for one reason: every kid deserves summer camp. SCOPE has been doing that work for years — turning a single summer at camp into a lifelong pathway through classroom, college, and career.

Every dollar of profit from our custom squeeze toys helps fund that pathway. We build the toys; SCOPE makes sure the kids get to camp — and stay supported long after they come home.
