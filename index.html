How It Works

Simple. Meaningful. Built by kids.

We partner with summer camps to design a custom squeeze toy unique to each camp — then sell them to fund more kids' camp experiences.

1

A Camp Joins

A camp partners with The Squeeze Project. A Camp Ambassador family commits to raising $2,250 — the one-time mold creation cost.

2

The Toy Is Designed

We design a custom squeeze toy inspired by the camp's identity. The mold is created by our factory partner — this typically takes about 6 weeks.

3

The Toys Are Sold

Toys are sold on our website, in camp stores, and through select retailers. Every sale supports the camp program.

4

Kids Go to Camp

100% of profits are used to help kids who need a little extra support get to the summer camp experience they deserve.
